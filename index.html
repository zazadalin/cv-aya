Experience

As a teenager, model scouts drew attention to me, and soon I made my debut on the podium and in front of camera lenses, shooting for advertising certain brands. While living in Almaty, I collaborated with the EAT Models agency. The modeling business fascinated me so much that as soon as I turned 16, I wanted to leave my native country for Paris or Milan and develop a career as a model there, because everyone around me predicted a great future for me in this industry.

Everything turned out differently: I gradually became interested in design, and soon I created my own brand of clothing in the style of minimalism "ASH". Modeling activity has moved to the background and has become one of my favorite hobbies. Already living in France, I participated in various shoots for glossy magazines. And once even appeared on the cover of the fashion magazine "ZUT!"
